About CaseConnect

Democratizing consulting interview preparation

Our Mission

At CaseConnect, we believe that exceptional consulting interview preparation shouldn't be limited to those with extensive networks or expensive coaching services. Our mission is to democratize access to high-quality case interview practice through peer-to-peer learning and expert mentorship.

Meet the Founder

Sarah Chen

Founder & CEO

Sarah is a former McKinsey consultant with 5+ years of experience in strategy consulting across technology, healthcare, and consumer goods sectors. After helping dozens of students prepare for consulting interviews, she realized the need for a more accessible and collaborative approach to case interview preparation.

She holds an MBA from Wharton and a BS in Computer Science from Stanford. When she's not building CaseConnect, you can find her mentoring students, hiking in the Bay Area, or experimenting with new coffee brewing methods.
