Frequently Asked Questions

What should I prepare before the session?

For case interviews, come with a pen and paper. For resume reviews, have your current resume ready. We'll send specific preparation instructions after booking.

Can I reschedule my session?

Yes, you can reschedule up to 24 hours before your session. Use the link in your confirmation email to make changes.

What if I need to cancel?

Cancellations made 24+ hours in advance receive a full refund. Cancellations within 24 hours are subject to a 50% fee.

Do you offer group sessions?

Yes! Group sessions are available for case buddy pairs at a discounted rate. Contact us for custom pricing.
